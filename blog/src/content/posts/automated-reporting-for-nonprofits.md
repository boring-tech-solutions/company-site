## FAQ

### What is automated reporting for nonprofits?

Automated reporting helps nonprofits organize program and participant data so reports for funders, boards, and stakeholders can be prepared faster and with less manual work.

### Why does funder reporting take so much time?

Many nonprofits receive funding from multiple grants and partners. Each funder may require different report formats, metrics, timelines, and supporting data.

### How does Govora help with nonprofit reporting?

Govora centralizes key organizational data so teams can more easily prepare funder, board, and program reports without relying on scattered spreadsheets and shared drives.
